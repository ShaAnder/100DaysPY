Tip Calculator Instructions

Create a tip calculator that Takes the total amount of the bill, adds a % tip on top (the user should choose between 5, 10, 15% tip)
then splits the bill by how many people were present and gives us the price per person rounded to 2 decimal places

Expected Format:

```
Welcome to the tip calculator!
What was the total bill? (Your bill)
How much tip would you like to give? 10, 12, or 15? 12
How many people to split the bill? 7
Example Output
Each person should pay: (Amount per person)
```
