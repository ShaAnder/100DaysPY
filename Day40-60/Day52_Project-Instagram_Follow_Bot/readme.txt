### --- PROJECT NAME / DESC --- ###

Instagram follower bot, this is a simple bot that will find a specific / similar person and follow their followers it's main intent is for learning
how to scroll down lists and continuously keep working


### --- PARAMETERS --- ###

Must be able to log into your fb and then insta account
must be able navigate to the account of the person you deem similar
must be able to open the follower list and follow all the accounts

### --- HOW IT WORKS --- ###

Much like the other selenium projects in this course it works but using scraping to get the various fields and then navigates through them
