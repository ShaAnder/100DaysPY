### --- PROJECT NAME / DESC --- ###

Auto Tinder Swiping bot, a bot simply put designed to swipe left or right on tinder.

### --- PARAMETERS --- ###

Must be able to log into your tinder account
must be able to close all popups and account for ads as they appear
must be able to swipe until cooldown or for 50 swipes


### --- HOW IT WORKS --- ###

WEll as stated the bot logs into your tinder account, swipes 
(only no as it's not really a project and i have no intention to use tinder) 
until it can't anymore or it hits 50 swipes

Due to how tinder has changed it's policies and how selenium has changed some things need to be done manually the first time around
- dealing with cookies on fb end
- cookies on tinder end
- enabling discovery mode
